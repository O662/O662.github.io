Design Intent

The house is organized around a protected courtyard that pulls daylight deep into the plan. Public spaces remain open and social, while private zones stay quiet and layered. Material choices focus on textured plaster, warm timber, and blackened steel accents.

Spatial Priorities

  • Morning light in the kitchen and dining areas
  • Outdoor rooms that extend living spaces
  • Future-proofed accessibility and adaptability
  • Acoustic separation for focused work

Program + Flow

Ground Level

Open living, kitchen, and dining wrapped around the courtyard. Sliding panels allow full or partial enclosure.

Upper Level

Primary suite, two bedrooms, and a shared studio with a north-facing light scoop.

Outdoor

Rain garden, shaded patio, and edible landscape supporting passive cooling.
