What We Do

David Turner Designer The Design Phase

We design in-house using CAD, and we work externally, when needed, with architects to produce beautiful, comfortable homes for today and for the future. As a Design Build Team, we take your new home concepts and apply them to the property dimensions, grade, location, setting, and view.

Working Together

The Building Process Pre-Construction

We work with you to establish a budget estimate which will incorporate the decisions you will make regarding quality, caliber, availability, and cost of the structure, materials, design features, and conveniences. A timeline is established, the permit process begins, and is followed by construction.

Why Choose Us?

David Turner 40+ Years Experience Construction

As an insured, bonded, licensed, home warranty builder, I am registered with BC Housing and offer 45 years experience as a building professional. Your home will be constructed to meet or exceed the BC Building Code, the Step Code, and it will include local seismic requirements.
